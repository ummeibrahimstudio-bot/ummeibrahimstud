Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order? ▼
Click "Order on WhatsApp" on any product page. A pre-filled message will open. Just send it and we'll reply within 24 hours with payment and delivery details. JazakAllah Khair!
Do you deliver all over India? ▼
Yes, Alhamdulillah! We deliver physically across all of India. Typical delivery time is 5–7 working days after payment confirmation.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash on delivery in select areas. Payment details are shared over WhatsApp after you place your order.
Can I return or exchange a product? ▼
If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please send us photos within 48 hours of delivery via WhatsApp and we will make it right, in sha Allah.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing? ▼
Yes! We offer special pricing for schools, Islamic centres, and bulk orders (10+ items). Please WhatsApp us directly with your requirements and we'll send you a quote.
Are the products suitable for gifting? ▼
Absolutely! Our products make beautiful Eid gifts, aqiqah gifts, and birthday presents for Muslim children. Just mention "gift" in your WhatsApp message and we'll take special care with packaging.
